Marvelous Soya Buns

Description

"Buns" typically refer to a type of bread roll that is soft and often slightly sweet. They can come in various forms and are used in a variety of culinary contexts. Here are some common types and uses of buns: Overall, buns are a staple in many cuisines, serving as a base for sandwiches, side dishes, or standalone treats.

Ingredients

  • Okara
  • Whole wheat flour
  • Date syrup
  • Baking Powder
  • Butter
  • salt
  • Vegetable oil

Making Procedure

  1. In a bowl combine flour, baking powder and salt together.
  2. In another bowl mix your okara, date syrup and butter very well.
  3. Scoop the wet mixture into the bowl containing dry mixture and mix until incoperated into a dough.
  4. Allow the dough to sit covered for about five minutes.
  5. cut and roll into small balls as the baking powder also acts as a rising agent
  6. Fry in a pre-heated oil enough to cover the rolls while frying.
