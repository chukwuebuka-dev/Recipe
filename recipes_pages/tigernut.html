Creamy and fluffy Tigernut milk drink

Description

Tigernut milk, also known as kunnu aya, is a beloved concoction that, in its most basic form, is made from just two ingredients: tigernuts and water.

ingredients

  • Tigernut seeds
  • Coconut
  • Dates
  • Clean drinking water
  • Ginger
  • Cinnamon powder

Making Process

  1. Grind the soaked tigernuts with ginger, dates, coconut, Cinnamon powder and water into fine texture to release the milk.
  2. Add more cold water and sieve to remove the chaff.
  3. Serve with ice or store in the refridgrator as it ferments very fast.
