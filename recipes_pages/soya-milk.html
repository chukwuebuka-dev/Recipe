Cranky Marvel Soya milk

Description

Soybean milk is a healthy alternative to cow's milk. Soybeans are composed of proteins, carbohydrates, fats, fiber, and a powerful array of phytonutrients.

Ingredients

  • Soya beans
  • Coconut
  • Dates
  • Ginger
  • Clean drinking water

Making Procedure

  1. Soybeans must be cooked in order to breakdown, a specific enzyme which makes them indigestible to humans.
  2. The cooked soya beans are then blended with sliced coconuts, dates, ginger and water in fine pulp
  3. This ground pulp is sieved using cheese cloth to extract the milk
  4. The milk is served chilled with ice cubes or bottled and stored in the refridgrator for later use.
